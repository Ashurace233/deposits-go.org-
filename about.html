About FDIC

Welcome to FDIC — a national initiative designed to inform and engage citizens about verified opportunities for financial assistance and community support programs across the United States. Our goal is to promote transparency, fairness, and accessibility in the distribution of public and privately funded awards that help individuals, families, and small businesses thrive. We are committed to ensuring every participant has equal access to legitimate opportunities and clear information on how to take part safely. As we look to the future, FDIC continues to expand its reach across multiple platforms — web, mobile, and social — driving engagement, loyalty, and growth in the evolving digital marketplace.
